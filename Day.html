Daikundi

Location & Geography:

Daikundi is a mountainous province in central Afghanistan, known for its highlands and scenic valleys. The capital city is Nili.

Culture & People:

The majority of the population are Hazaras, who have a rich cultural heritage, including traditional music, storytelling, and handicrafts.

Traditional Food:

Popular local dishes include Ashak (dumplings filled with vegetables or meat) and Qormeh (meat stew).

Traditional Clothes:

Men often wear perahan tunban (long shirt with pants) and women wear colorful dresses with headscarves, sometimes with traditional embroidery.

Cultural Events:

Festivals like Nowruz (Persian New Year) and local harvest celebrations are widely celebrated.
